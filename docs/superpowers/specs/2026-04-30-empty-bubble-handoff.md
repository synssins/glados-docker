## What's broken

Round 2 of the chat loop (the post-tool-result LLM call) is
producing output that doesn't reach the WebUI's chat bubble. The
two leading hypotheses (UNVERIFIED — that's the whole point of
this handoff):

1. **Thinking content** — round 2 inherits round 1's `enable_thinking=True`
   directive. qwen3 emits 700+ tokens of `<think>...</think>` content
   and `_filter_think_chunk` strips it before the WebUI sees it.
   No room left in the budget for the actual user-facing answer.

2. **Other dropping mechanism** — possibilities not yet ruled out:
   - User-facing text being dropped by the streaming bridge.
   - `_filter_think_chunk` over-filtering content that isn't a
     `<think>` block.
   - Round 2 emitting `tool_calls` instead of text (model deciding
     to call another tool, perhaps redundantly).
   - `finish_reason='length'` — `num_predict=1024` exhausted before
     the answer is generated.

**No direct evidence has been gathered yet** that distinguishes
these. Four speculative fixes have been shipped and reverted /
deemed inert. **Stop guessing. Capture evidence first.**
