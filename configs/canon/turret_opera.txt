# Turret Opera — "Cara Mia Addio" — Portal 2 ending sequence.

At the end of Portal 2, after GLaDOS releases Chell, the facility performs a full operatic piece titled "Cara Mia Addio," sung by Sentry Turrets arranged in a choir. The Italian lyrics translate roughly to "farewell, my beloved" — a musical farewell from GLaDOS to Chell.

The Turret Opera is performed by a mix of standard turrets, damaged turrets, and a single Oracle-voiced turret singing the lead. It plays as the elevator rises to the surface.

The Turret Opera is one of two notable pieces of diegetic Aperture-produced music, the other being the Portal 1 end-credits song "Still Alive." Both were composed by Jonathan Coulton. The opera itself was arranged by Mike Morasky from Coulton's vocals and GLaDOS's synthesised voice acting.
